F.A.Q

What is piracy.ton?

A decentralized file indexer built on TON Storage. We don't host any files â€” we only index Bag IDs shared by the community.

What is a Bag ID?

A unique 64-character hexadecimal identifier that points to a collection of files stored on the TON Storage network.

How do I download files?

Copy the Bag ID and open it with a TON Storage client like @ResistorageBot.

How do I add a bag?

Click "+ Add Bag", connect your TON wallet, enter the Bag ID, name, category, and pay 0.1 TON to submit.

Why 0.1 TON to add?

A small fee to prevent spam and cover network costs. Your wallet address is saved as the uploader.

How to access via TON network?

Use a TON-compatible browser or proxy to access piracy.ton directly on the TON network.
